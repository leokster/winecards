# Privacy Policy for WineCards

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2024

## Introduction

WineCards is a flashcard application designed to help users study wine knowledge. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Do NOT Collect:
- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No location data
- No usage analytics
- No advertising identifiers
- No user accounts or authentication data
- No network requests to external servers

## Data Storage

### Local Data Only:
- **Starred Questions**: When you mark questions as favorites, this preference is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.
- **Question Content**: All flashcard questions are bundled with the app and stored locally.

### No Cloud Sync:
- Your starred questions are only stored on your device
- Data is not synced to iCloud or any other cloud service
- Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

## Third-Party Services

WineCards does NOT use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics frameworks
- No advertising networks
- No crash reporting tools
- No social media integrations

## Image Loading

The app displays images from the following domain:
- `elearning.academie-du-vin.ch`

These images are loaded directly from the server and are not stored on our servers. No tracking or data collection occurs during image loading.

## Children's Privacy

WineCards does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for educational purposes related to wine knowledge.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your device's built-in security protects your data
- iOS security features apply (device encryption, Face ID/Touch ID, etc.)
- No data transmission means no risk of data breach from our servers

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Delete Data**: Uninstall the app to remove all data
- **Reset Data**: Delete and reinstall the app to start fresh
- **No Account Required**: No registration or account creation needed

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
