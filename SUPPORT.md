## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: What is WineCards?**
A: WineCards is a flashcard study app for WSET Level 2 wine certification preparation. It contains questions and answers to help you study wine knowledge.

**Q: Is WineCards affiliated with WSET?**
A: No, WineCards is an independent study tool and is not officially affiliated with or endorsed by the Wine & Spirit Education Trust (WSET).

**Q: Does the app require an internet connection?**
A: The app works offline. However, an internet connection is required to load wine label images in some questions.

### Using the App

**Q: How do I star/favorite a question?**
A: Tap the yellow star icon at the bottom of the screen while viewing a question. Starred questions can be viewed by tapping the "Markiert" button at the top.

**Q: How do I see only my starred questions?**
A: Tap the "Markiert" button at the top of the screen to view a list of all your starred questions. Tap any question to study it.

**Q: How do I get a new random question?**
A: You can:
- Tap the "Nächste Frage" button at the bottom
- Swipe left anywhere on the screen
- The question will animate out and a new one will appear

**Q: Can I go back to a previous question?**
A: The app shows random questions. To review specific questions, star them first, then access them from the "Markiert" view.

**Q: How do I remove a starred question?**
A: In the "Markiert" view, tap the star icon next to the question to unstar it. Or, when viewing a starred question, tap the star button to unstar it.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Images are not loading. What should I do?**
A:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try closing and reopening the app
3. If images still don't load, some questions may contain placeholder text like "[Bild]"

**Q: The app crashed or froze. What should I do?**
A:
1. Force close the app (swipe up from app switcher)
2. Restart the app
3. If problems persist, try restarting your device
4. As a last resort, delete and reinstall the app (your starred questions will be lost)

**Q: My starred questions disappeared. Why?**
A: Starred questions are stored locally on your device. They may be lost if you:
- Delete and reinstall the app
- Restore your iPhone from a backup that didn't include app data
- Clear app data through Settings

**Q: The app is slow or laggy. What can I do?**
A:
1. Close other apps running in the background
2. Restart your device
3. Ensure your iOS is up to date
4. Free up storage space on your device

**Q: Swipe gesture not working. What should I do?**
A: Make sure you're swiping left on the question/answer area, not on the buttons. Swipe at least 50 pixels to trigger the gesture.

### Data and Privacy

**Q: Does the app collect my personal data?**
A: No. WineCards does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

**Q: Are my starred questions synced across devices?**
A: No. Starred questions are stored locally on each device and are not synced.

**Q: Can I export my starred questions?**
A: Currently, there is no export feature. This may be added in future updates.

**Q: What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?**
A: All local data, including your starred questions, will be deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Troubleshooting Steps

### App Won't Open:
1. Check for iOS updates
2. Check for app updates in the App Store
3. Restart your device
4. Delete and reinstall the app

### Questions Not Displaying:
1. Force close and reopen the app
2. Check for app updates
3. Ensure adequate storage space
4. Reinstall the app if necessary

### Gestures Not Working:
1. Ensure you're not in the starred questions list view
2. Swipe with more force/distance
3. Check for any accessibility settings that might interfere
4. Restart the app
